Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Your order details will be securely sent to our team via WhatsApp for instant confirmation.

How is my order confirmed?

Once you send the WhatsApp order message, our team will review the availability and confirm your order along with the payment details.

Are all products always in stock?

We maintain real-time inventory, but occasionally highly demanded items may run out. If an item becomes unavailable after placing a request, our team will notify you immediately via WhatsApp.

How fast is the delivery?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. Exact timelines will be shared upon order confirmation.

Do you accept exchanges?

Yes, we accept size exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unworn and in its original condition. Please read our Return & Exchange policy for more details.

Shipping Policy

  • Delivery Locations We deliver pan-India to all serviceable pincodes. If your location is outside our standard delivery zones, we will notify you during order confirmation.
  • Estimated Delivery Time Standard orders are typically delivered within 3-5 business days. Remote locations may take up to 7-10 business days.
  • Shipping Charges We offer FREE shipping on all standard orders. Any exceptions or expedited shipping requests will be communicated clearly before final payment.
  • Order Processing Time All confirmed orders are dispatched within 24 hours of successful payment verification (excluding Sundays and public holidays).

Return & Exchange Policy

  • Exchange Eligibility We offer size exchanges to ensure a perfect fit. The item must be unused, unwashed, and retain all original tags and packaging.
  • Time Limits Exchange requests must be raised via WhatsApp within 7 days of receiving the delivery.
  • Return Conditions We currently only support exchanges for sizing issues or defective products. Direct monetary refunds are not provided unless the item received was fundamentally defective and a replacement is unavailable.
  • Non-Returnable Items For hygiene reasons, undergarments and custom-tailored bulk group orders cannot be returned or exchanged.

Privacy Policy

At AK Fashions, we highly respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

  • Data Collection We only collect essential information required to fulfill your order, which includes your Name, Mobile Number, Delivery Address, Pincode, and District.
  • Data Usage Your information is exclusively used for order processing, providing customer support, and communicating important updates regarding your purchases.
  • Third-Party Sharing We maintain strict confidentiality. Your personal information will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except for our trusted logistics partners when strictly necessary to deliver your order.
